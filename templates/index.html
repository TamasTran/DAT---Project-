Chu thich cac benh

  • Cassava Bacterial Blight (CBB)

    Benh thoi chon do vi khuan Xanthomonas gay loang bac tren la, lam he than bi nut va co the lam heo canh.

  • Cassava Brown Streak Disease (CBSD)

    Virus CBSV/UCBSV lam gan la sam den, la bi xoan va khoai bi soc nau, khi nang se ton that tinh bot nghiem trong.

  • Cassava Green Mottle (CGM)

    Benh nam dom xanh nhat do CGMV gay bien dang la, giam quang hop va thuong lan nhanh neu khong loai bo la nhiem som.

  • Cassava Mosaic Disease (CMD)

    Nhom virus kham la gay hoa tiet vang xanh, la xoan nho lai va cay suy yeu, giam nang suat dao dong 20-90%.
